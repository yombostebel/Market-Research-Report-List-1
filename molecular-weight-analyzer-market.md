<p><strong>The Molecular Weight Analyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gel Permeation Chromatography (GPC)</li><li>Gas Chromatography & Mass Spec (GC/MS)</li><li>Size Exclusion Chromatography (SEC)</li><li>High Performance Liquid Chromatography (HPLC)</li><li>Differential Refractive Index (DRI)</li><li>Multi-Angle Laser Light Scattering (MALLS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Molecular Weight Analyzer market encompasses various techniques for determining molecular weight and size distribution of polymers and biomolecules. Gel Permeation Chromatography (GPC) separates molecules based on size, while Size Exclusion Chromatography (SEC) relies on similar principles for macromolecules. Gas Chromatography & Mass Spectrometry (GC/MS) combines gas chromatography with mass analysis for volatile compounds. High-Performance Liquid Chromatography (HPLC) offers high resolution for complex mixtures. Differential Refractive Index (DRI) detects changes in refractive index, and Multi-Angle Laser Light Scattering (MALLS) measures light scattering to determine molecular weight.</p></p>
