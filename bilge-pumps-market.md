<p><p>The bilge pumps market consists of two primary types: electric bilge pumps and manual bilge pumps. Electric bilge pumps are powered by electrical systems, offering automated operation for efficient water removal from a vessel's bilge, making them popular for larger boats. In contrast, manual bilge pumps rely on physical effort to function, providing a fail-safe option during power outages or for smaller crafts. Both types are essential for maintaining vessel safety and preventing water accumulation, contributing to overall maritime reliability.</p></p>
